Welcome on the website for the Benelux Algorithm Programming Contest (BAPC). The BAPC 2016 is hosted by the  Delft University of Technology and will take place on the 22nd of October.  

The event will start from 9:00. The actual contest will take place from 13:00-18:00. For more details please take a look at the Schedule.

Registration is possible between Monday the 26th of September and Sunday the 7th of October.
