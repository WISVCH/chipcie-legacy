CHipCie Hall of Fame
The CHipCie (Christiaan Huygens Important Programming Committee) is the committee of Study Association 'Christiaan Huygens' responsible for organising all programming contests at the faculty for Electrical Engineering, Mathematics and Computer Science of the Delft University of Technology.
