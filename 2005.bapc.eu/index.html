Benelux Algorithm Programming Contest sponsored by Sogeti
Formerly known as 'Nederlands Kampioenschap Programmeren' or 'NKP'
Welcome to the site of the Benelux Algorithm Programming Contest (BAPC) sponsored by Sogeti.
On this site you can find information about the BAPC. On the left side you can choose a subject and view more information about it. Currently, the BAPC is being organised and is scheduled to be held Saturday the 22nd of October 2005. Any university or college from the Netherlands, Belgium and Luxembourg can enter the contest with one or more teams. The contest will be held in Delft and will take all day, including the drink and the dinner at the end. As more details about the BAPC become available, be assured that they'll be added to this website. If you have any questions, be free to send us an e-mail.
When?
Saturday, 22 october 2005
What can you win?
First of all you can win the title "Benelux Champion Algorithm Programming". Student teams can win €512 (1st place), €256 (2nd place) and €128 (3rd place). The business teams compete for the price of €128 and the title "Benelux Algorithm programming business champion".
