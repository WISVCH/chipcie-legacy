Accommodation

There are multiple (mostly small) hotels available in the neighbourhood of Delft. We have selected a hotel at which we have arranged a preferred offer. The prices are outlined below. The reservations have to be done by the teams themselves via direct contact with the hotel. To get the preferred offer, please read the instructions below. If you would like to search a hotel on your own, you are free to do so (for example via bookings.com).

WestCord Hotel Delft

Website: http://www.westcordhotels.com/hotels/hotel-delft

This large modern business hotel is located convenient near the exit of the motorway A13. There are parking spaces available in the parking lot in front of the hotel. The price of the hotel excludes breakfast, although this can be booked as an option for €13,50 per person. The price per double room is €79,- per night (excluding €2,30 taxes per person per night). There is an option to add a third bed to the double rooms. If you would like to book this hotel, a reservation code can be obtained during the registration on this site or by sending us an email.
