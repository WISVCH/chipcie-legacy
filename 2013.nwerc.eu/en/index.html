NWERC 2013 is integrated in the ACM International Collgiate Programming Contest. It draws students from colleges and universities throughout Belgium, Luxembourg, Great Britain, Ireland, Iceland, Norway, Sweden, Finland Denmark, Germany and the Netherlands. The winning team will advance to the ACM Programming Contest World finals. In the world finals teams will compete with other student teams from other regional contests that have taken place on all continents.
