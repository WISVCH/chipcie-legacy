NWERC Preliminary contest

The NWERC has four official preliminary contests. Teams that want to participate in the NWERC are not required to participate in the regional, but it is a good opportunity to practice and scout the competition. Some universities use the preliminary contest to select teams if more teams are interested in participating.

Benelux Algorithm Programming Contest (BAPC)

Other Preliminaries

Did you host a local contest to select teams for your university? We like to know about them and register participation numbers. These numbers are taken into account when determined the number of teams that can advance to the World Finals.
