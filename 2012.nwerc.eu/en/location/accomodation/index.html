Accommodation

There are multiple (mostly small) hotels available in the neighbourhood of Delft. We have selected two hotels at which we have arranged a preferred offer. The prices are outlined below. The reservations have to be done by the teams themselves via direct contact with the hotels. To get the preferred offer, please read the instructions of your selected hotel below. If you would like to search a hotel on your own, you are free to do so (for example via bookings.com).

The hotels that we provide a preferred offer to are outlined below.

WestCord Hotel Delft

Website: http://www.westcordhotels.com/hotels/hotel-delft

This large modern business hotel is located convenient near the exit of the motorway A13. There are parking spaces available in the parking lot in front of the hotel. The price of the hotel excludes breakfast, although this can be booked as an option for €13,50 per person. The price per double room is €79,- per night (excluding €2,30 taxes per person per night). There is an option to add a third bed to the double rooms. If you would like to book this hotel, a reservation code can be obtained during the registration on this site or by sending us an email.

Hotel Juliana Delft

Website: http://www.hoteljuliana.nl/

Hotel Juliana is located near the contest site. A single room costs €75,90 per night which includes breakfast but excludes €2,30 tax. A double room costs €93,60 per night and again includes breakfast but excludes €2,30 tax per person. If you would like to book this hotel, you are advised to contact the hotel directly and mention that you are reserving a room for the NWERC 2012, after which the hotel will give you the discount. This offer is valid until 1 November 2012.
