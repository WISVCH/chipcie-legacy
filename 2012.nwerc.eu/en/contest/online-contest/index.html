Online contest

During NWERC 2012, there will be an online contest for everyone who wants to participate as a spectator team. It is possible to participate in both the test session and the main contest.

  • The online test session will start at 10:45 on Saturday 24th of November.
  • The online version of the main contest will start at 9:45 on Sunday 25th of November.
